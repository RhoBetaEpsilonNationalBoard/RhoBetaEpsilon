About us

Rho Beta Epsilon is a national robotics engineering honors society, founded at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in 2007. Rho Beta Epsilon strives to encourage and recognize exemplary robotics students while aiding in their development both personally and professionally through exceptional education, leadership, and passion for robotics as well as to enable the advancement of the robotics engineering community.
